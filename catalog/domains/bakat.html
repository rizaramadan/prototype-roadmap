Person Domain

bakat.id

The person's permanent career identity — portable across employers, owned by the individual.

URLbakat.id ModelSingle-tenant (person-centric) Identity keyHashed National ID Sub-appssiswa · beasiswa · award

🪪 Overview

bakat.id holds the individual's profile, portable assessment history, and serves as the consent control plane for cross-platform data sharing.

💡

Portability principle: Data earned at one employer (e.g., Learning Agility score at Client A) is retained in bakat.id and travels with the person. When they join Client B, they authorize that client to read it — data is never trapped inside a client's tenant.

🔐

Consent model: bakat.id is where people manage consent. Each platform that wants to read a person's retained data must be explicitly authorized by the person. Consent is scoped (e.g., "allow Client B's peningkatan.id to read my Core Behaviour history") and revocable at any time.

⚙️ Services

Distinct capabilities within bakat.id, all sharing the same person profile as foundation.

ProfileService

Core person profile management. Stores identity, Core Behaviour history, and portable assessment results. Entry point: www.bakat.id.

publishes events

ConsentService

Manages per-platform consent grants and revocations. Emits consent events consumed by data-provider platforms.

publishes events

PortabilityService

Retains assessment results from ases.id into the person's portable profile. Responds to ConsentGranted by exposing retained data to authorized platforms.

consumes events

SiswaService

Student-specific features on siswa.bakat.id. Tracks academic profile, learning progress, and connects to partner platforms (edulab, AI Visor).

publishes events

BeasiswaService

Scholarship management on beasiswa.bakat.id. Handles applications, eligibility, and award disbursement with institutional partners.

publishes events

AwardService

Recognition and achievement tracking on award.bakat.id. Issues verifiable awards and interfaces with institutional partners (e.g., intikom).

publishes events
