Recruitment Domain

recru.id

Multi-tenant ATS and job portal — manages the full hiring funnel from job posting to hire, delegating assessment to ases.id.

URL<client>.recru.id ModelMulti-tenant Event isolationPer-tenant namespace ActorsRecruiter · Pencari Kerja (job seeker)

💼 Overview

recru.id manages job postings, candidate pipelines, and the handoff to ases.id for assessment — then hands off successful hires to peningkatan.id.

🔀

recru.id does not do assessments. When a candidate reaches the assessment stage, recru.id issues an AssignAssessment command to ases.id and waits for PersonCoreBehaviourProfileUpdated. The recruiter sees the fit score in recru.id — but the measurement always happens in ases.id.

🌉

Two candidate entry points: Candidates apply directly via <client>.recru.id, or they originate from an external ATS (e.g., ats.ClientX.com) and are bridged in via the ATSBridgeService. Both paths converge at ases.id.

🏢 Multi-tenancy Model

AspectBehaviour
DeploymentEach client gets <client>.recru.id. Talentlytica also runs a managed public instance at recru.id.
Event namespaceAll events carry a tenantId. Events from clientA are invisible to clientB.
Candidate dataCandidate data is tenant-scoped. The same person applying to two clients appears as separate records within each tenant.
Cross-tenant identityThe hashed national ID allows Talentlytica to recognize the same person across tenants at the platform level — not exposed to individual clients.

🌉 External ATS Bridge

For clients with their own ATS (e.g., ats.ClientX.com), the ATSBridgeService acts as an anti-corruption layer.

1
Candidate applies on external ATS
The client's own ATS collects the initial application. Identifies candidates by email or other identifier.
2
Webhook → ATSBridgeService
External ATS posts candidate data (email, job context) to recru.id's bridge endpoint. Bridge translates into a recru.id candidate record.
3
ases.id AssignAssessment
ATSBridgeService delegates to AssessmentDelegationService, which sends the assessment invite via ases.id.
4
Results delivered back to external ATS
When PersonCoreBehaviourProfileUpdated is received from ases.id, ATSBridgeService sends the fit score back to the external ATS via its webhook. Client ATS receives: candidate identifier + fit score.
