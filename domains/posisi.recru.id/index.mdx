posisi.recru.id answers: **"What does this position actually require?"** It breaks every role into its constituent Core Behaviours, enabling precise matching, career path design, and targeted assessment.

**Core Behaviour ontology:** Atom (Core Behaviour) → Molecule (Competency) → Compound (Position Profile). This enables mix-and-match career pathing: two positions sharing 70% of Core Behaviours can be bridged by a targeted IDP.

posisi.recru.id is Talentlytica's native equivalent of O*NET and SkillsFuture — but Core Behaviour-native.
