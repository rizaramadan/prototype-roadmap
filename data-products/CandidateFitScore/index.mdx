## Overview

The Candidate Fit Score is a single [0.0–1.0] number that answers: **"How well does this candidate's Core Behaviour profile match what this position requires?"**

It is computed by ScoringService immediately after `AssessmentCompleted`, using:
1. The candidate's `coreBehaviourScores` from Assessment Results
2. The position's `coreBehaviourRequirements` (minScore + weight) from posisi.recru.id

A score of 1.0 means every required Core Behaviour meets or exceeds the threshold at weight. A score below a configurable tenant threshold auto-advances the candidate to `rejected`.

## Consuming services

- **HiringDecisionService** — displays score to recruiter before hire/reject decision
- **ApplicationService** — drives automatic stage advancement past threshold
- **ATSBridgeService** — pushes score to external ATS on request

## Freshness

Written within seconds of `AssessmentCompleted`. Immutable once written.
